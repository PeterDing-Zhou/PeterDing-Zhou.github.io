    Wuyi Mountain

    As a major symbol of southeastern China and one of the UNESCO World Heritage Sites, Mount Wuyi is highly regarded as the place where Cheng-Zhu Neo-Confucianism originated and developed. Since the 11th century, Cheng-Zhu Neo-Confucianism has had far-reaching influence in East Asia. Bamboo rafting in the low valley of the Jiuqu River is very popular with tourists. It lasts for 2 hours and the journey of 8 kilometers allows you to enjoy the magnificent scenery of Wuyi. To appreciate the gentle mountain peaks and clear river water of Wuyi, rafting is definitely the best choice.

Gansu

Mingsha Mountain and Crescent Moon Spring, Dunhuang City

Mingsha Mountain, which surrounds Crescent Moon Spring, consists of a series of small hills. Mingsha Mountain is famous all over the world for its unique shape and the characteristic of singing with the wind. The resounding echoes of the hills can be heard as the wind blows over the hills. Visitors can ride camels up the 250-meter-high hill. The blooming flowers in the garden beside Crescent Moon Spring form an interesting contrast with the surrounding yellow sand.

Guangxi

Yangshuo

Yangshuo, a waterfront town in southern China, is well known for its many Custer hills and traditional fishing village lifestyle. In the tourist area of the city center, tourists can rent a bicycle and go after the soothing scenery: bamboo rafts drift slowly along the river, fishermen gently release their fishing cormorants, and farmers in the fields The fields are set amidst towering lush mountains.

Guizhou

Huangguoshu Waterfall

As the tallest waterfall in Asia, the magnificent Huangguoshu Waterfall is about 77.8 meters high and 101 meters wide. It is also the largest large waterfall in the world, and its scenery can be seen from almost all directions. The best tourist season is from June to August, when the rapids can reach a maximum speed of 700 cubic meters per second.

Hainan ⛱

Yalong Bay

In Hainan, you can experience the most beautiful tropical style in China, and Yalong Bay is the most beautiful among the most beautiful. Yalong Bay is a crescent-shaped beach that stretches for 7.5km. It is the most popular and prosperous beach on the coastline at the southern end of Hainan. Here, you can indulge in all the best things about a Southeast Asian vacation. Because Yalong Bay has both tall and lush palm trees and luxurious high-star hotels—more than 20 luxury hotels with international standards are scattered on the beach. The sandy beach is also a paradise for water sports lovers. Here from sunrise to sunset, surfers can ride the wind and waves and overcome obstacles.

Hubei

Shennongjia

Over the past 100 years, more than 400 people have claimed to have seen Bigfoot in the jungles of Shennongjia, but no conclusive evidence has yet been found. Nurturing more than 5,000 kinds of animals and plants, the nature reserve covers an area of 3,200 square kilometers, and has the most complete subtropical forest ecosystem in the mid-latitude region. It is also the home of the golden monkey, a rare and protected animal in China.

Zhangjiajie

In fact, director James Cameron was inspired by the limestone pillars of Wulingyuan, which led to the floating mountains on the planet Pandora in the Oscar-winning work "Avatar". In fact, there are more than 3,000 such stone pillars in the Wulingyuan Stone Forest in Zhangjiajie, Hunan Province, the highest of which is 400 meters high. It is said that the Wulingyuan authorities have named one of the stone pillars "Hallelujah Mountain" based on the name of the main peak of Pandora Fushan.

Jilin

Changbai Mountain Tianchi

The Changbai Mountain National Nature Reserve is close to the border with North Korea, and there are 16 peaks in the reserve. Tianchi is a piece of jasper inlaid among the peaks. With an average water depth of 204 meters, Tianchi is the deepest lake in China. It is also a hotspot for water monster lovers. It is said that in the past 20 years, travelers have found water monsters as long as 20 meters in the lake.

Sichuan

Jiuzhaigou

Charming mountain lakes and waterfalls can be seen everywhere in Jiuzhaigou. The huge lake in the picture above is the crown jewel of Jiuzhaigou. The magic of the lake water is that it can change color day and night year after year. The color of the lake comes from the reflections of the lakeside scenery, as well as the algae and calcified rocks on the bottom of the lake. Autumn is the best time to visit Jiuzhaigou. At this time, the lake surface is colorful and colorful, which is comparable to a painter's colorful palette.

Tibet

Tibet Potala Palace

The nine-story Potala Palace stands at an altitude of 3,600 meters, making it the highest palace in the world. It was once the residence of the Dalai Lama's Winter Palace and the seat of the former ruling government of Tibet. The current identity of the Potala Palace is the National Museum. There are more than 1,000 existing houses in the palace, while retaining the original layout of the White House and the Red Palace. The White House is the residence of the Dalai Lama, while the Red Palace is the cultural center of Tibetan Buddhism.

Xinjiang

Karakule Lake

The breathtaking view above is a nice reward after a thrilling ride on the Karakoram Highway - the most dangerous road in the world. Tourists generally set off from Kashgar, the westernmost city in China, to Karakule Lake - the numerous "landslide sites" warning signs along the way cannot stop their infatuation. The lake is located on the Pamir Plateau above 3,600 meters above sea level.
